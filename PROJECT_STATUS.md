## 🔧 Known Limitations

### Minor Issues (2% remaining)
- ⚠️ No user authentication (planned for v2.0)
- ⚠️ No video clip extraction (planned for v2.0)
- ⚠️ No export to PDF/SRT (planned for v2.0)
- ⚠️ No real-time streaming support (planned for v2.0)
- ⚠️ No mobile app (planned for v2.0)

### Performance Considerations
- Large videos (>1GB) may take significant time to process
- CPU-only processing is slower than GPU
- Memory usage scales with video length
- Disk space needed for uploads and frames
